Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NexusFlow

Do I need coding skills to use NexusFlow?

No coding required! NexusFlow features an intuitive drag-and-drop builder that anyone can use. However, if you want advanced customization, we support custom code blocks too.

Can I try NexusFlow for free?

Yes! We offer a completely free Starter plan with up to 100 workflows per month. No credit card required. You can upgrade to Professional anytime with a 14-day free trial.

How long does setup take?

Most teams are up and running in under 10 minutes. Simply connect your apps, choose from our pre-built templates or create your own, and launch your first workflow.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level AES-256 encryption, are SOC 2 Type II certified, and comply with GDPR. Your data is stored in secure data centers with 99.9% uptime SLA.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription anytime with no penalties. Your workflows will continue running until the end of your billing period, and you can export all your data.

Do you offer customer support?

We provide email support for all users. Professional and Enterprise plans get 24/7 priority support via email, chat, and phone. Enterprise customers also get a dedicated account manager.

What integrations do you support?

We support 5,000+ integrations including Salesforce, Slack, Gmail, Shopify, HubSpot, Zapier, and many more. Can't find what you need? Contact us for custom integrations.

Can I migrate from another automation tool?

Yes! We offer free migration assistance for teams switching from other platforms. Our team will help you import your workflows and get you up and running quickly.
